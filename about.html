Our story

Started in a kitchen too small for what we wanted to do

Food Fudge began as a six-table cafe because we couldn't find a place nearby that treated breakfast and dinner with the same care. So we built one ourselves.

How we got here

A short history, in real steps

Nothing about Food Fudge happened overnight — here's the order things actually unfolded in.

2014

Six tables, one oven

We opened with a single wood-fired oven and a menu of eleven items. Most mornings, the owner was also the one taking orders.

2017

The brownie that named us

A brown-butter fudge brownie recipe, refined over two years, became the dish regulars asked for by name — and eventually gave the cafe its name.

2020

Partnering with local growers

We moved to sourcing produce from six growers within a short drive, rebuilding the menu each season around what they had.

2023

Forty seats, same kitchen ethos

We expanded into the space next door, growing to forty seats without changing how anything is cooked.

Today

Still a neighborhood kitchen

The tables are still close enough to hear your neighbor's conversation. We like it that way.

What we hold to

A few things we don't compromise on

🌱

Sourced close to home

We buy from growers we've met, not catalogs. If a vegetable isn't in season nearby, it isn't on the menu.

🔥

Made from scratch, daily

Bread, stocks, and sauces are made in-house every morning. Nothing arrives frozen and gets reheated.

🤝

A seat that's yours

We don't turn tables fast. Once you're seated, the table's yours for as long as you want it.
