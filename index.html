Our story

Started in a kitchen too small for what we wanted to do

Food Fudge opened as a six-table cafe because we couldn't find a place nearby that treated breakfast and dinner with the same care. So we built one — sourcing from growers we know, baking before the sun's up, and keeping the menu small enough that everything on it is good.

Years on, the tables are still close enough to hear your neighbor's conversation. We like it that way.
